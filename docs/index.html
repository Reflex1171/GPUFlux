How It Works

One command. Cheapest GPU.
Every time.

GPUFlux is an open-source CLI that plugs into every major GPU provider, finds the best price for your workload, and deploys it automatically.

01

Define your workload

Specify GPU type, VRAM, Docker image, and budget in a simple YAML file. Takes 30 seconds.

02

GPUFlux finds the best deal

We query every provider in real-time, compare pricing, availability, and reliability scores across regions.

03

Deploy automatically

One command provisions the cheapest matching instance, deploys your job, and starts monitoring. Done.

04

Stay optimised

Coming soon: auto-migration when cheaper capacity appears, failover on termination, and spend tracking.
